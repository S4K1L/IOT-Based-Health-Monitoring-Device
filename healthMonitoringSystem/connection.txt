Pulse Oximeter (MAX30100):
VCC: Connect to 5V on Arduino Nano
GND: Connect to GND on Arduino Nano
SDA: Connect to A4 on Arduino Nano
SCL: Connect to A5 on Arduino Nano

GPS Module:
RX: Connect to D9 on Arduino Nano (TX pin on GPS module)
TX: Connect to D10 on Arduino Nano (RX pin on GPS module)
VCC: Connect to 5V on Arduino Nano
GND: Connect to GND on Arduino Nano

SIM800L GSM Module:
RX: Connect to D3 on Arduino Nano
TX: Connect to D2 on Arduino Nano
VCC: Connect to 5V on Arduino Nano
GND: Connect to GND on Arduino Nano

Switch:
Connect one side to D5 on Arduino Nano
Connect the other side to GND on Arduino Nano

I2C LCD (LiquidCrystal_I2C):
SDA: Connect to A4 on Arduino Nano
SCL: Connect to A5 on Arduino Nano
VCC: Connect to 5V on Arduino Nano
GND: Connect to GND on Arduino Nano
